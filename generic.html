Debicheck

Debicheck is an electronic debit order system in South Africa designed to enhance the security and efficiency of recurring payments. It aims to simplify the debit order process for both consumers and businesses, ensuring greater control and transparency in debit transactions.
KEY FEATURES OF DEBICHECK Authorization Process , Real-Time Notifications , Reversal Process, Standardized Format , Integration with Banking Systems
BENEFITS
Enhanced Customer Trust , Reduced Fraud , Operational Efficiency , Improved Cash Flow Management. We had 3 types of transaction TT1 (Real time through messages) , TT2 (batch processing) , TT3 (Real time authentication through USSD) So basically three phases were involved; Prepayment; The mandate part, payment: the collection part and post payment includes bank error, dispute i.e. after the settlement has been done.
